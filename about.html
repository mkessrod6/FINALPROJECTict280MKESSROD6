Our Story

From a shared passion for the planet to a fast-growing sustainability tech company.

Our Mission

Technology That Protects the Planet

Founded in 2019 in Albuquerque, NM, GreenTech Solutions was born from a simple belief: that every business, regardless of size, should have access to the tools needed to operate sustainably.

We design, build, and deploy eco-friendly technology that helps organisations measure, reduce, and offset their environmental impact — without sacrificing productivity or profitability.

Our mission is to accelerate the transition to a low-carbon economy by making sustainability software and hardware accessible, affordable, and genuinely useful.

  • 🌱
    Planet First Every product decision starts with its environmental impact. If it's not greener, we don't ship it.
  • 💡
    Radical Transparency We publish our own carbon data and hold ourselves to the same standards we set for clients.
  • 🤲
    Inclusive Access Sustainability shouldn't be a luxury. We price and design for SMBs, not just enterprise giants.
Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

Five years of growth, innovation, and measurable climate impact.

2019

Company Founded

GreenTech Solutions incorporated in Albuquerque, NM. Seed funding of $1.2M secured to develop our first carbon tracking prototype.

2020

Carbon Tracker Beta Launch

Released Carbon Tracker v1.0 to 25 beta clients. Reduced collective emissions by 400 tonnes CO₂ in the first six months. Won the NM Clean Energy Innovation Award.

2021

SmartBin Hardware Line

Launched our first hardware product — the SmartBin — in partnership with 3 major New Mexico municipalities. Series A funding of $4.5M closed.

2022

100th Business Client

Crossed 100 active business clients. Expanded the team to 30 employees and opened a second office in Denver, CO.

2023

National Recognition

Named to Fast Company's "Most Innovative Companies" list in the Sustainability category. Surpassed 10,000 tonnes of CO₂ reduced across all clients.

2024–25

500+ Clients & Growing

Serving over 500 businesses nationwide. Launched EcoDesk Hub, the industry's first AI-powered smart workstation. Carbon-neutral company certification achieved.
